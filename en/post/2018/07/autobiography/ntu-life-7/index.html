At the beginning, I was full of passion. But later I gradually realized that browser research is truly hard. Browser software development seems to have reached a plateau, and no one can say for sure whether it is already near the ceiling. Put simply: it is hard to achieve major breakthroughs; progress tends to be incremental. I think the biggest difficulty is the complexity of browsers themselves.
